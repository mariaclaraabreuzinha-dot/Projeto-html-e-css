Shopify is an e-commerce platform that allows you to create an online store to sell physical or digital products. It works by providing ready-made tools to add products, manage orders, accept payments, and handle shipping—all from an easy-to-use dashboard. You choose a theme, customize your store design, and start selling online.

Shopify operates on a monthly subscription with different plans depending on the features you need. It usually offers a free trial, and after that you can choose a plan for your store. Additional costs may include paid themes, apps, or transaction/payment fees depending on your setup.
